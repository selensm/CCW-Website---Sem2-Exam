How we got to where we are today...

CCW started back in 2015 as a traditional car wash center based in Aalborg. Soon enough a group of loyal customers who would return for more of our services was formed, which encouraged us to set up a car wash based on subscription so that we could provide our loyal customers what they need on a reasonable price.

… and now we are only growing bigger!

In 2019 our subscription packages became so popular that we had to find a new and larger workshop and CCW moved to it’s current location. The same year we decided to expand our company and reach more people in Denmark, so we opened gates for the citizens of Aarhus.
